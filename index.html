How We Work

It is a simple issue of supply and demand. In 2016, California had severe draughts, so garagiste and small label winemakers were buying whatever they could afford to make excellent wine. In 2017, when the drought ended, there were massive influxes in grapes and winemakers were buying up more grapes. Unfortunately, even though the drought ended, no one is getting richer, so they can't sell their wine for premium prices without dropping their own price. So Hollow Wines was born! We can buy premium sustainable California Wine and sell it to you at a fraction of the cost!
